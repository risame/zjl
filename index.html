Jiale Zhang is a lecturer in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. He received his master's degree in Computer Technology from Tianjin Polytechnic University in 2017. Then, he joined College of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, and received his Ph.D degree in 2021. In 2018, he studied in School of Computer Science at University of Technology Sydney as a visiting student. His research interests include AI security and privacy, federated learning, blockchain, mobile edge computing, software security, etc. He has published about 25 papers in international journals and conferences. He is a CCF and IEEE member.
